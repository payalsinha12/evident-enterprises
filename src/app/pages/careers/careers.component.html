BPO Career Opportunities in Property Damage and Solicitor Assistance

Legal Customer Service Representative

Industry: BPO / Property Law

Assist clients seeking legal advice related to property damage, mold issues, and legal claims. Schedule consultations with solicitors and provide ongoing support.

House Damage Inspection Coordinator

Industry: Property Inspection / BPO

Coordinate with property inspectors to assess house damages (e.g., mold, structural issues). Ensure all documentation is collected and clients are informed.

Legal Assistant (Claims & Damage Inspections)

Industry: Property Law / BPO

Assist solicitors in reviewing damage claims, inspecting reports, and managing client communication regarding house damage cases.

Client Relations Specialist (Damage Claims)

Industry: BPO / Property Claims

Maintain communication with clients throughout the property damage claims process. Provide status updates and ensure clients' needs are addressed.
